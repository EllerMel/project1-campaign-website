About

Buffy Anne Summers was a Slayer that was activated in the late 20th century. Born on January 19 1981, she was called to be the Slayer in 1996 at the age of fifteen. Buffy was originally living in Los Angeles; but, due to an incident involving a gang of vampires at Hemery High School, she moved to Sunnydale with her mother.
